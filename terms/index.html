What Yield is

Yield is a free desktop utility that reads time entries from your Harvest account and assignments from your Forecast account and shows them in the macOS menu bar. You can also create, edit, pause, resume, and delete Harvest time entries from the app. Yield runs entirely on your Mac and talks directly to the Harvest and Forecast APIs — there is no Yield-operated server, account, or backend.
